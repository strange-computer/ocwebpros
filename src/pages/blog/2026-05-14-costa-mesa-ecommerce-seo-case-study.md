## The 6-Month Costa Mesa E-commerce SEO Strategy

### Month 1-2: Technical Foundation & Localization

#### Phase 1: Technical SEO Audit & Fixes
We conducted a comprehensive technical audit focusing on e-commerce-specific issues:

**Technical Issues Found:**
- **Page speed:** 3.8 second load time (Google target: < 2 seconds)
- **Mobile usability:** 12 mobile compatibility issues
- **Duplicate content:** 47% of product pages had duplicate manufacturer descriptions
- **Broken links:** 23 broken internal links
- **Missing schema:** No product or local business schema markup
- **Crawl errors:** 15 important pages blocked by robots.txt

**Technical Fixes Implemented:**
1. **Page speed optimization:**
   - Image compression (reduced from 450KB to 80KB average)
   - Lazy loading implementation
   - CSS/JS minification and combination
   - CDN implementation for faster Orange County delivery

2. **Mobile optimization:**
   - Responsive design fixes
   - Mobile-specific image sizes
   - Touch-friendly navigation
   - Accelerated Mobile Pages (AMP) for product pages

3. **Duplicate content resolution:**
   - Rewrote 142 product descriptions with unique, SEO-optimized content
   - Added manufacturer descriptions as collapsed sections
   - Implemented canonical tags for variant pages

#### Phase 2: Costa Mesa Local SEO Foundation

## Lessons for Costa Mesa E-commerce Businesses

### Lesson 1: Local Beats Global in E-commerce
Even in e-commerce, local relevance matters. Costa Mesa shoppers prefer businesses that understand their local context.

### Lesson 2: Technical SEO is Non-Negotiable

Local address, phone, pickup options, and delivery information significantly increase conversion rates.
