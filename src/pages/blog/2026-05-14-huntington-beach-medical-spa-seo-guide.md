## HIPAA-Compliant Medical Spa SEO

### What HIPAA Allows for Medical Spas:
- **Educational content** about procedures
- **Generic before/after photos** (with authorization)
- **Service descriptions** without PHI
- **Office information** and team bios
- **Testimonials** (with written authorization, no PHI)

### What to Avoid:
- **Patient names** or identifiable information
- **Specific treatment details** without authorization
- **Appointment information** or medical history
- **Before/after photos** without written consent
- **Treatment results** tied to specific individuals

### HIPAA-Compliant Content Strategy:

#### Procedure Education:

## Before/After Gallery Optimization

### HIPAA Compliance Protocol:
1. **Written consent forms** for every photo

## Review Generation and Management

### Why Reviews Matter for Medical Spas:
- 91% of patients read online reviews before booking
- Medical spas with 4.5+ stars get 3x more consultation requests
- 78% trust reviews as much as personal recommendations
- Reviews significantly impact local pack rankings

### HIPAA-Compliant Review Strategy:

#### Asking for Reviews:
- **In-office:** After successful treatment, provide review card or tablet
- **Email:** Follow-up email 2-3 days post-treatment
- **Text:** Simple text with review link (opt-in required)
- **Never:** Ask for reviews mentioning specific treatments or results

#### Review Platforms for Medical Spas:
1. **Google** (most important for local search)
2. **Yelp** (very important for Huntington Beach)
3. **RealSelf** (aesthetic-specific platform)
4. **Healthgrades** (medical-specific)
5. **Facebook** (social proof and community)
6. **RateMDs** (medical professional reviews)

#### Review Response Strategy:
- Respond to every review within 48 hours
- Thank patients generically: "Thank you for choosing our medspa!"
- Never acknowledge specific treatments: "We appreciate your feedback about your experience."
- Take negative reviews offline immediately: "We take your feedback seriously. Please contact our office manager."
- Maintain professional medical tone in all responses

#### Review Generation Prompts:
- "How was your overall experience with our team?"
- "Would you recommend our medspa to friends or family?"
- "How was your consultation and treatment process?"
- "What did you think of our office environment?"
(Always generic, never treatment-specific)
