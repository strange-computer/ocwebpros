## Service Area Business (SAB) Optimization

### Understanding Service Area Businesses:
- No physical storefront address for customers
- Service area based on ZIP codes or cities
- Home-based or office-based with service radius
- Different Google Business Profile requirements

### Google Business Profile Setup for Contractors:

#### Business Name:
**Right:** "[Company Name] - Orange County [Service]"
**Wrong:** Just company name (misses service and location)

#### Service Area Specification:
- **Cities served:** List all Orange County cities you serve
- **ZIP codes:** Specific ZIP code coverage areas
- **Neighborhoods:** Major neighborhoods or communities
- **Radius:** Mile radius from your base location

**Example Service Area:**
"Serving Orange County including Irvine, Newport Beach, Costa Mesa, Huntington Beach, Fountain Valley, and surrounding areas. We cover ZIP codes 926XX, 927XX, and 928XX."

## Review Generation and Management

### Why Reviews Matter for Contractors:
- 97% of homeowners read online reviews
- Contractors with 4.5+ stars get 3x more calls
- 79% trust reviews as much as personal recommendations
- Reviews significantly impact local pack rankings

### Contractor Review Strategy:

#### Asking for Reviews:
- **After job completion:** When customer is satisfied
- **Email follow-up:** 2-3 days after project completion
- **Text message:** Simple request with review link
- **In-person request:** With thank you card or final walkthrough
- **Never:** Before job completion or when issues exist

#### Review Platforms for Contractors:
1. **Google** (most important for local search)
2. **Yelp** (very important for Orange County)
3. **HomeAdvisor** (contractor-specific)
4. **Angi** (formerly Angie's List)
5. **Facebook** (social proof and community)
6. **Nextdoor** (neighborhood recommendations)

#### Review Response Strategy:
- Respond to every review within 24 hours
- Thank customers specifically: "Thank you for choosing us for your [project]!"
- Address concerns professionally: "We'll contact you to resolve this."
- Highlight project details: "We enjoyed working on your [specific project]."
- Maintain professional contractor tone

#### Review Generation Prompts:
- "How was your experience with our team?"
- "Would you recommend us to neighbors or friends?"
- "What did you think of our communication and timeliness?"
- "How was the quality of our work?"
