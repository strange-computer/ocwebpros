## Step 1: Complete Irvine-Specific GBP Setup

### Business Name Optimization:
**Right:** "OCWebPros - Irvine SEO Agency"
**Wrong:** "OCWebPros" (too generic)

Include your primary service + "Irvine" in your business name. This helps Google understand your location relevance immediately.

### Categories Selection:
Choose categories that match Irvine search behavior:

**Primary Category:** Most specific to your service
**Secondary Categories:** Add Irvine-relevant variations:
- Tech services: "IT Services", "Tech Support", "Computer Repair"
- Professional services: "Business Consultant", "Marketing Agency", "SEO Service"
- Retail: "Clothing Store", "Electronics Store", "Home Goods Store"

### Service Area Setup:
Be specific about which Irvine areas you serve:

**Example for service business:**
- Irvine Business Complex (92612, 92614)
- University Park (92612)
- Irvine Spectrum (92618)
- Woodbridge (92604)
- Northwood (92620)

**Pro Tip:** Use neighborhood names in your description, not just ZIP codes. Irvine residents identify strongly with neighborhoods.
