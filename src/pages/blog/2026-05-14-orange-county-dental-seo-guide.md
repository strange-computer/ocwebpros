## HIPAA-Compliant Dental SEO: The Foundation

### What HIPAA Allows (and Doesn't Allow):

**Allowed:**
- Patient testimonials (with written authorization)
- Before/after photos (with written authorization)
- General service descriptions
- Educational content about procedures
- Office hours, location, contact information

**Not Allowed:**
- Protected Health Information (PHI) without authorization
- Patient names/faces without authorization
- Specific treatment details without authorization
- Appointment details or medical history

### HIPAA-Compliant Content Strategy:

#### Educational Content:
- "What to Expect During a Root Canal"
- "Dental Implants vs Dental Bridges: Which is Right for You?"
- "Invisalign vs Traditional Braces: A Complete Comparison"

## Before/After Gallery Optimization

### HIPAA Compliance:
- Written authorization for every photo
- No patient names in file names or alt text
- Generic descriptions only
- Consider digital alteration for privacy

### SEO Optimization:
- Descriptive file names: "before-after-dental-implants-orange-county-01.jpg"
- Alt text: "Before and after dental implants case study at Orange County dental practice"
- Captions: "Case study showing dental implant results. Individual results may vary."

## Review Generation and Management

### Why Reviews Matter for Dentists:
- 94% of patients read online reviews
- Practices with 4+ star ratings get 2x more calls
- 68% of patients trust reviews as much as personal recommendations

### HIPAA-Compliant Review Strategy:

#### Asking for Reviews:
- **In-office:** After successful treatment, provide tablet or card with review link
- **Email:** Follow-up email 1-2 days after appointment
- **Text:** Simple text with review link
- **Never:** Ask for reviews mentioning specific treatments or PHI

#### Review Platforms for Dentists:
1. **Google** (most important)
2. **Yelp** (very important for Orange County)
3. **Healthgrades** (medical-specific)
4. **Zocdoc** (appointment platform)
5. **Facebook** (social proof)
6. **RateMDs** (medical reviews)

#### Review Response Strategy:
- Respond to every review within 48 hours
- Thank patients generically: "Thank you for choosing our practice!"
- Never acknowledge specific treatments: "Thank you for your feedback about your visit."
- Take negative reviews offline: "We'd like to discuss your experience further. Please call our office."

#### Review Generation Prompts:
- "How was your experience with our team?"
- "Would you recommend our practice to friends and family?"
- "How was your wait time and appointment experience?"
(Always generic, never treatment-specific)
